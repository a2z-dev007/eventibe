Company Details
Homocation Asia Private Limited
(Parent Company of Eventibe.com & VenueForEvent.com)

1. Corporate Overview
Homocation Asia Private Limited is an Indian hospitality technology and event marketplace company committed to building structured, scalable, and technology-driven platforms within the hospitality and event ecosystem. The company operates and manages two specialized digital brands:

Eventibe.com
VenueForEvent.com
Both platforms are designed to simplify venue discovery, event planning, and vendor connectivity through a structured, inquiry-driven marketplace model.

Homocation Asia Private Limited is legally incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013 (India) and operates with a long-term vision of creating integrated hospitality and event technology ecosystems across India and international markets.

3. Background & Foundational Vision
While the legal incorporation took place in 2024, the conceptual foundation of the company dates back to 2012. The founders identified a persistent gap in the hospitality and event industry:

Event hosts struggled to find verified venues with structured information.
Venue owners lacked consistent digital exposure.
Vendors depended heavily on offline references.
Corporate planners faced inefficiencies in sourcing event infrastructure.
The vision was simple but powerful:

To create a structured digital marketplace that bridges venues, vendors, and event hosts through transparent discovery and inquiry-based engagement.

This philosophy forms the core of Homocation Asia Private Limited and all its brands.
